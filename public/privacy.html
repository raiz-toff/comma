Privacy Policy for COMMA

Last Updated: May 16, 2026

COMMA ("we", "our", or "us") is a local-first application designed for gig workers to track their earnings and expenses. Your privacy is our priority.

1. Data Collection & Storage

Local Storage: COMMA is built on a "local-first" architecture. All your financial data, shift logs, and settings are stored locally on your device's browser database (IndexedDB). We do not collect, store, or transmit your financial data to our own servers.

2. Google Drive Backup

If you choose to enable the "Cloud Backup" feature, COMMA will request access to your Google Drive App Data Folder.

  • Restricted Access: We only access the hidden "App Data" folder created specifically for COMMA. We cannot see, edit, or delete any of your other personal files in Google Drive.
  • Purpose: This folder is used exclusively to store your encrypted vault backups so you can sync your data across your own devices.

3. Third-Party Services

COMMA does not sell or share your data with any third parties. The application interacts directly with Google APIs for backup purposes if enabled by the user.

4. Your Rights

Since your data is stored on your own device and your own Google Drive, you have full control over it. You can delete your local data at any time via the "Reset Vault" option in Settings, or delete your backups directly from your Google Account.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@rajkumarneupane.com.
